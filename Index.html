About Us

Gather is an innovative platform designed to connect students to the plethora of activities and social networks available on campus. Our mission is to enhance the student experience, foster community engagement, and contribute to holistic personal development. With a focus on inclusivity, diversity, and student well-being, Gather is not just an app but a community where every student belongs and thrives.
